Biography

Hongyu Chen is a senior undergraduate majoring in statistics at School of Mathematical Sciences, Peking University. He also takes a double major in economics at National School of Development, Peking University. His research interest lies in the field of Operations Management, with particular emphasis on online marketplace, including market design, supply chain finance, etc. He has the pleasure to be supervised by Zizhuo Wang, David Simchi-Levi, and Weiming Zhu during his undergraduate research.
